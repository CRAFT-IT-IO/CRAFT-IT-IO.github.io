The Fact Framework is a sophisticated solution that’s ready for us to customize for you. It’s a flexible and fully configurable modular framework used by banks, financial institutions and corporates across the globe.

The Fact Framework can be completely integrated into your existing workflow, incorporating spreading and financial analysis, risk rating, loan origination and approval workflow processes. Limit and exposure management and covenant monitoring modules are available too.

The Fact Framework enables your organization to comply with general Basel requirements, providing a platform for consistent, auditable credit and rating processes.
