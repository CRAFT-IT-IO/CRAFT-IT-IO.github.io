Moody’s Analytics and CRAFT IT company announced their partnership to offer best-of-breed technology, implementation, and consulting solutions that help clients to respond to their financial and credit risk operational needs by providing first-class full services globally.

Moody's proven, end-to-end solutions provides financial intelligence and analytical tools to help business leaders make better, faster decisions, while managing risk, streamlining processes, and complying with internal policies and regulations over the credit life cycle.
